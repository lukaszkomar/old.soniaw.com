Sonia Warzyńska

She began her musical education at the age of seven, playing violin. She finished vocal studies in the Academy of Music in Cracow under the guidance of dr hab. Maria Seremet in 2014.

She made her opera debut at State Opera House in Crocow as Susanna in student’s performance of Le Nozze di Figaro. Shortly after she sang Hanna Glawari’s role in The Merry Widow of Franz Lehar in Wroclaw. Besides, in her repertoire is for example Giulietta from Capuleti e i Montecchi or title role of Händel’s Rodelinda.

Many times she performed solos for masses, oratories and cantatas, for example: Requiem i Mass in C minor of Mozart, Haydn’s Nelson Mass, Oratorio de Noël by Saint-Saens. She has also given recitals in Poland: in Cracow, Wroclaw, Gdansk and Sopot.

She is laureate of international and countrywide singing competitions. In 2013 she won special prize for the best interpretation of a classical aria at the 3rd International Giulio Perotti Singing Competition in Ueckermünde (Germany), in 2014 a second prize at 6th Countrywide Karol Kurpiński Singing Competition in Włoszakowice (Poland) and a second prize at 6th Countrywide Vocal Competition Impressio Art Sopot (Poland). In 2015 she get a Honourable mention at 16th Ada Sari International Vocal Artistry Competition in Nowy Sącz (Poland).

She improved her skills during many masterclasses taught by: Rudolf Piernay, Mariusz Kwiecień, Ryszard Karczykowski, Paul Esswood, Rosemarie Landry, Piotr Kusiewicz and Jerzy Artysz.
