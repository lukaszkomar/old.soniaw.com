SINGET:



SINGET came into being in 2011, on the initiative of the graduates and students of the Academy of Music in Kraków. The management of the entire undertaking has been taken over by Janusz Wierzgacz.

Due to a wide variety of the members’ temperaments the band easily settles in at any type of music. However, the main objective is to popularize a capella pieces, as well as the oratorio and cantata music of the forgotten classicist and romanticist composers.

The band’s core is build on 10 people and is expanded with some constantly cooperating singers according to the repertoire. Each of the artists is competent to perform solo parts if required by a given piece. SINGET can boast with the premiere performance of some works at the Omnia Beneficia composers’ competition in Stary Sącz and the „Owoce życia” cycle by E. Popławski within the composer’s concert at the Academy of Music in Kraków or the performance of four recently discovered manuscripts of Austrian composers’ masses from the 19th century.
